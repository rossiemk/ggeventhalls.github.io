Welcome to Kingstine-Jo Foods

Kingstine-Jo Foods is a leading fast food company with a promise to deliver full value for money to an increasingly discerning target audience. Over the years, We have set and maintained high quality and excellent service standards in the Quick Service Restaurant business. Our company pioneered the integration of African menu into fast food operations. We invite our customers to experience more as they interact with our brand and enjoy the sensory pleasures of our sizzling hot chicken and sides. All flavoured to perfection with our unique blend of authentic West African herbs and spices. We encourage our staffs and franchisees to passionately learn, grow and strive for more, both to their own benefit and to the ultimate benefit of our customers. On this basis, We pride ourself on the consistent delivery of product excellence, great value and impeccable customer service. All brought to life by warmly engaging with our customers in energetic, progressive, quick service restaurant environment. At Kingstine-Jo Foods our products are freshly prepared to order, to ensure that they are served hot and delicious every time! Our food is soulfully spiced with a blend of authentic West African spices and flavored with love. Plus, we only use high quality ingredients.

Catering Services

We offer to you, our first class catering service for your events hosted at Great God Events Hall or at your selected event locations.

Meal Delivery Service

We know there are those days when you wish someone would just bring that food direct to your door step, hot and ready to eat. This is why we have created this service to meet you at your point of need. We deliver to all locations around our branches. Free delivery for orders above N5,000. Please note, orders below N5,000 will come with a food delivery charge.


24/7 Service

Kingstine-Jo Foods team is never too busy to get meals across to customers. We are available for Breakfast, Lunch, and even Dinners and could be used for Office, Events, Conferences, Conventions, Workshop, e.t.c. Our operation runs Monday to Friday (8am-8pm) and Saturdays & Sundays (9am-9pm).

Eat-in and Take away

You have the option to enjoy your meals and delicacies at our restaurant, we have gone to good lengths to make you feel welcomed and entertained or you could take your favourite dishes away with you in our take out packs.
